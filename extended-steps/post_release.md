## Step: Post-Release

**Description**  
Monitor the production environment after deployment, manage any incidents that arise, and conduct a retrospective to capture lessons learned and drive continuous improvement.

---

### Inputs
- **Production Deployment** (completed)
- **Monitoring Dashboards and Alerting Configuration**
- **Incident Management Process**
- **Service Ownership Documentation** *(PM-28)*

---

### Output
- Post-release monitoring enabled and active *(REQ-28)*
- Monitoring logs and dashboards available
- Incidents logged and analyzed *(REQ-29)*
- Incident reports produced
- Retrospective conducted and actions tracked *(REQ-30)*
- Retrospective report with actionable items
- Operational ownership formally handed over *(PM-28)*
- Project closure report *(PM-29)*
- Lessons learned document shared with organization *(PM-30)*
- Post-deployment smoke test results *(DEP-02)*
- Production monitoring and alerting active *(DEP-03)*
- Centralised logging operational *(OPS-01)*
- ICT incident tickets logged and tracked *(OPS-02)*
- Root cause analysis documents for major incidents *(OPS-03)*
- Test evidence archived per retention policy *(AUD-03)*

---

### Governance
- Post-release monitoring dashboards configured and active *(REQ-28)*
- All incidents logged, analyzed, and resolved *(REQ-29)*
- Retrospective conducted after each release cycle *(REQ-30)*
- Action items from retrospective tracked to completion *(REQ-30)*
- Operational ownership formally handed over to Service Owner *(PM-28)*
- Project closure is formally approved by Sponsor *(PM-29)*
- Lessons learned are documented and shared across the organization *(PM-30)*
- Post-deployment smoke tests executed *(DEP-02)*
- Monitoring and alerting enabled in production *(DEP-03)*
- Centralised logging implemented for incident detection and forensic analysis *(OPS-01)*
- ICT incidents are logged and tracked *(OPS-02)*
- Root cause analysis performed for major incidents *(OPS-03)*
- Test evidence retained according to policy *(AUD-03)*

---
