## Step B2: Scope, Planning & Stakeholder Management

**Description**  
Define and approve the project scope, establish the project plan with milestones and resources, and set up stakeholder management processes. This step ensures that all planning activities are properly governed and that stakeholder communication is structured, supporting the EU AI Act's emphasis on transparency, proportionality, and effective oversight.

---

### Inputs
- **Project Charter** (from Step B1) *(BUS-01)*
- **Criticality Classification** (from Step B1) *(CRT-01)*
- **Governance Model** (from Step B1) *(PM-03)*
- **Business Case** (from Step B1) *(BUS-03)*

---

### Output

#### Scope Management
- Approved scope statement with clearly defined boundaries *(PM-05)*
- Scope exclusions document listing out-of-scope items *(PM-06)*
- Change management plan defining the change process *(PM-07)*
- Change request log with formal approval workflow *(PM-08)*

#### Planning
- Approved project plan with milestones *(PM-09)*
- Stakeholder-agreed schedule with realistic timeline *(PM-10)*
- Resource plan with identified and approved resource needs *(PM-11)*
- Approved and baselined budget *(PM-12)*

#### Stakeholder Management
- Stakeholder register with identification and mapping *(PM-17)*
- Communication plan defining reporting cadence and channels *(PM-18)*
- Status report template and schedule *(PM-19)*
- Escalation procedure known to all stakeholders *(PM-20)*

---

### Governance
- Project scope clearly defined and approved by Steering Committee *(PM-05)*
- Out-of-scope items explicitly documented *(PM-06)*
- Change management process defined and agreed *(PM-07)*
- Scope changes require formal approval through change request *(PM-08)*
- Project plan and milestones approved by Sponsor *(PM-09)*
- Timeline is realistic and agreed by all stakeholders *(PM-10)*
- Resource needs identified, approved, and allocated *(PM-11)*
- Budget approved and baselined by Sponsor *(PM-12)*
- Stakeholders identified and mapped *(PM-17)*
- Communication plan defined and distributed *(PM-18)*
- Regular status reporting schedule established *(PM-19)*
- Escalation process defined and communicated *(PM-20)*
